A.P.J Abdul Kalam

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam BR was an Indian aerospace scientist and statesman who served as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born and raised in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu and studied physics and aerospace engineering. He serves as my role model because of his hardwork and dedication. This outstanding man worked consistently to improve the lives of countless people and did wonders for his nation. Throughout his life, APJ Abdul Kalam worked hard and accomplished many wonderful things.

If you fail, never give up because FAIL means "First Attempt In Learning.

-A.P.J Abdul Kalam
